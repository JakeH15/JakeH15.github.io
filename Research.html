Research Interests

I am interested in understanding the evolution of ('complex') life. I am currently working with the Snowflake yeast system. I have one main project in the works: what role do sedimentation regimes play in the Snowflake yeast system. Other questions and projects that I am interest in are: under which conditions are non-clonal clusters preferred over conal clusters (the classic "coming together" vs "sticking together") and modeling the growth period of Snowflake yeast be as a markov chain (this ends up being a graph theory problem).
