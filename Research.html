Research Interests

I am interested in how cells respond and adapt to their environment and what role physics plays in this process. I am currently working with the snowflake yeast system (clonal groups of S. cerevisiae) in Dr. Peter Yunker's lab at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I have one main project in the works: Effect of Hydrodynamic Regime on Snowflake Yeast Evolution. Other questions and projects that I am interested in are: under which conditions are non-clonal clusters preferred over conal clusters (the classic "coming together" vs "sticking together") and modeling the growth period of snowflake yeast as a branching process.
