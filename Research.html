Research Interests

I am interested in understanding the evolution of ('complex') life. I am currently working with the Snowflake yeast system to discover the modes and tempos at which multicellular life evolved. I have two projects in the works currently: what role do sedimentation regimes play in the Snowflake yeast system and under which conditions are non-clonal clusters preferred over conal clusters (the classic "coming together" vs "sticking together"). In addition to these projects that I am activley working on, I would like to model the growth period of Snowflake yeast as a markov chain (this ends up being a difficult graph theory problem) and I would like to apply Jonathan's work on hierarchial structures to evolution of hierarchial structures in biology. This would be a melding of Dan McShea's work with Jonathan's.
