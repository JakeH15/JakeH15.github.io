Summer Updated, 1-21-19

I ended last semester with the best grades I've had since starting at Tech. I still got one C (I've gotten a C every semester now), but the rest of my grades were As. I was extremely fortunated to recieve funding through Dr. Yunker to perform 10 weeks of research at a full time pay over the course of the summer. At the end of the spring semester, I went home for a week before heading to collegiate nationals. I had an incredibly poor performance in the time trial and crashed out of the road race with a broken bike and broken thumb. After nursing my wounds and ego for a couple days, I went back up to Atlanta for the 2019 Microbial Games Workshop and the inaugrual Evolution of Complex Life conferece, where I presented my research as a poster presentation. After attending those events, I headed back to Savannah, GA for a week to help my parents work on the house. They are trying to sell it and have been slowly repainting the extorior.

I finally started working, three weeks after the semester ended, and have been at it since. At the end of this week I will take off for Maine to visit my dad for his birthday. I haven't made as much progress as I would of liked with the research because of contamination, but things are going steadily forward.

I have started studying for the GRE and the Physics subject test, and I have started searching for potential Phd advisors. I plan on graduating in the Spring and if things work output starting a Phd program Fall 2020.
