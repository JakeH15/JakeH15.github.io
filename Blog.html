Bike Racing, Research, and School, 1-21-18

It's almost halfway through the semester, and the bike racing season is in full swing. So far, I have race four times for Welland Racing and four times Georgia Tech Cyling. The Georgia Tech Cycling team is full of really good people this year and I've been having a blast traveling around the Southeast with them. There are a few grad students on the team who do work in the fields of machine learning, bioinformatics, and fluids, and I have really enjoyed talking with them about their fields and the ways in which they overlap with what I am doing in my research project. I haven't had any results yet, but the legs are still coming into form and hopefully the results will follow shortly. The goal this year is to qualify for the collegiate national road championships that are being held close to home in Augusta, GA.
